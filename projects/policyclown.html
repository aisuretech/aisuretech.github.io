🤡 Policy Clown

AI-Powered Satirical Political Predictions

Policy Clown turns daily political chaos into absurd entertainment. It combines breaking headlines with documented political stances and uses AI to generate exaggerated, satirical policy forecasts. The result is a daily political parody feed that is intentionally over-the-top and designed purely for entertainment.
