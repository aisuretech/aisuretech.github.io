🎭 Trump Derangement Syndrome (TDS) Watch

Satirical AI-powered daily publication exploring disproportionate commentary

Overview

Trump Derangement Syndrome (TDS) is a cultural and rhetorical term used to describe commentary or reactions to Donald Trump that are disproportionate, hysterical, or factually unmoored. TDS Watch is a satirical, AI-powered daily publication brought to you by PolicyClown.com and focused on mock-scientific political comedy.
