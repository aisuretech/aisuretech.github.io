🏛️ Presidential Assassination Attempts Explorer

A Searchable Archive of Known Threats Against U.S. Presidents

Presidential Assassination Attempts Explorer is a searchable, data-driven archive of every known attempt, plot, and incident targeting U.S. presidents from 1835 to today. Each entry includes historical context, a confidence rating, and details on the target, method, and outcome. Visitors can explore the material through filters, an interactive timeline, a geographic map, and charts that reveal long-term patterns. An AI assistant modeled on a presidential history scholar answers natural language questions, compares incidents, and explains broader context.

What You Can Do

  • Search every known attempt, plot, and incident targeting U.S. presidents from 1835 to today
  • Filter the archive by president, era, method, outcome, and confidence rating
  • Explore incidents through an interactive timeline, geographic map, and pattern-revealing charts
  • Read historical context for each entry with details on the target, method, and outcome
  • Compare incidents across presidents and time periods to spot long-term trends
  • Ask an AI presidential history scholar natural-language questions about specific events or broader patterns
