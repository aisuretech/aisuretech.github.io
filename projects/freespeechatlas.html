🗽 Free Speech Atlas

History, Law, and Debate Around the First Amendment

Free Speech Atlas is an educational hub that explains the history, law, and major debates surrounding free speech and the First Amendment. It breaks down landmark cases, censorship disputes, campus speech issues, social media moderation, and AI-era expression in clear, accessible language. The site features an AI assistant, a timeline of key events, and updated news coverage, giving readers a single, non-partisan place to understand where speech stands today.
